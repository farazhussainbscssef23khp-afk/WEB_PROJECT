How It Works

Take a Photo

Capture the problem with your camera - broken streetlights, potholes, garbage overflow, or any public issue.

Add Location

Provide the exact location of the problem. Use our location detection or enter the address manually.

Describe the Issue

Write a brief description of the problem to help authorities understand and resolve it quickly.

Track Progress

Monitor the status of your report and see how authorities are working to resolve the issue.

About FixIt

FixIt is a civic engagement platform that empowers citizens to report public problems and infrastructure issues in their communities. Our goal is to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local authorities, making it easier to maintain and improve public services.

Whether it's a broken streetlight, pothole, garbage overflow, or any other public issue, FixIt provides a simple and effective way to report problems and track their resolution. Together, we can make our cities cleaner, safer, and more efficient.
